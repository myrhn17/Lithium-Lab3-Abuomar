Roblox

Roblox is an online platform where players can create, play, and share their own games. It has tons of different games made by users such as adventures, simulations, and obbies. You can explore these games, meet other players world wide, and costumize your character. It's a creative platform where you can let your imaginations go wild.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:
  • You are able to connect with your friends even when apart.
  • Multiple games are stored that you can choose from whenever you are bored.
  • It doesn't require skills, just have fun.
  • You can costumize your avatar whenever you want.
  • You can make a game yourself and earn robux from it.
